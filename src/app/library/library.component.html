LIBRARY

About Library:

Bharathiar University PG Extension and Research center Library is fully equipped to meet essential academic and intellectual need of its students with user friendly.
This College has Central library and Department libraries. It is well stocked and the book collection consists of more than 10,000 books. Collection is continuously updated with changes in curriculum. Working hours



Working Hours:

The library is opened all working days in a week. Timings are 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM. It remains closed on all Saturday, Sundays and Government Holidays.

Library services


College Library is providing the following services.

  • Reference service
  • Lending services (Book issue and return)
  • News paper clipping service
  • Literature search service
  • Journal service
  • Library orientation programme to First year students
  • Display of new arrival books
  • Conduct of book exhibitions to university
  • Library notice board service
